About Us

Founded in 1995, Wheaton Shower Glass Company began with a vision to revolutionize bathroom safety and aesthetics. What started as a small family business has grown into Wheaton's premier provider of custom shower glass solutions, serving thousands of satisfied customers throughout the region.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional quality and service while maintaining the highest standards of safety and craftsmanship. We believe in transparency, integrity, and putting our customers first in everything we do. Our core values of excellence, innovation, and reliability guide every installation and repair we perform.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation typically take?

Most standard installations are completed within 4-6 hours. Custom projects may take 1-2 days depending on complexity.

What type of warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive lifetime warranty on all glass and installation work, covering both materials and labor.

How do I maintain my shower glass?

We recommend daily squeegee use and weekly cleaning with our specialized glass cleaner. We provide detailed care instructions with every installation.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency repair services for existing customers, with most repairs completed within 24 hours.

What are your payment options?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and offer flexible financing options with approved credit. Deposits are required for custom installations.
